About Me

As a curious UX Researcher and lifelong student of human behaviour, I believe technology should be a support, not a burden.

I'm committed to finding deeper ways to understand what people truly need. Here is the philosophy that drives my research:

  • 🧠 Investigating Cognitive Load — People are already juggling a million things; their tools shouldn't add to the noise. I identify points of mental friction and advocate for cognitive and social distribution, finding ways the system and community can share the mental effort.
  • ⚖️ Championing Ethical Tech — Good research is inherently ethical. I advocate for design decisions that respect user privacy, autonomy, and well-being — helping teams avoid dark patterns and build solutions that are genuinely helpful, accessible, and honest.
  • ✨ Inspiring Serendipity & Joy — I don't just find out what's broken; I discover what brings people joy. By delivering empathetic, nuanced insights, I empower teams to create moments of serendipity that build lasting product loyalty.
